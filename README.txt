== Description ==

This CloudNet360 eCommerce plugin allows users to automatically synchronize their CloudNet360 small business sales automation account products to their WordPress database.

WordPress site owners can easily showcase their CloudNet360 products on any page or location of their WordPress site by copying and pasting a simple short code [cloudnet_products_showcase] to seamlessly leverage the CloudNet360 sales platform from their WordPress site.


Major features in The CloudNet360 eCommerce system:

* Automatically updates all products to your WordPress site.
* Works with all the powerful up sell functionality in Cloudnet360. 
* Point and click simplicity for product management.
* Integrates videos (and images) for each product to boost conversions.
* Credit card security is handled by CloudNet360 for you.
* Sophisticated product “preview” features your customers expect.
* Membership sites are a breeze to create and manage.

Your WordPress site brings the traffic, the CloudNet360 plugin converts that traffic into eCommerce gold.


== Installation ==

Upload the CloudNet360 plugin to your blog, Activate it, then enter your [CloudNet360 Sync.com API key](http://www.cloudnet360.com/).

1, 2, 3: You're done!
